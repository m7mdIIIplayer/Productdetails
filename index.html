Experience the exquisite flavor profile of our Ethiopian Yirgacheffe coffee beans, grown in the highlands of southern Ethiopia. These beans are known for their bright acidity, complex flavor notes, and floral aroma that coffee connoisseurs around the world cherish.

Our Yirgacheffe beans are carefully sourced from small-scale farmers who use traditional methods to grow and harvest their crops. The beans are then medium roasted to perfection to highlight their natural characteristics while developing a balanced body.

Tasting Notes

  • Floral and citrus aroma
  • Bright, wine-like acidity
  • Notes of bergamot, lemon, and jasmine
  • Smooth, medium body
  • Clean, sweet finish with hints of chocolate

Perfect For

These beans shine in pour-over methods like Chemex or V60, which highlight their complex flavor profile. They also make an exceptional single-origin espresso with a bright, vibrant character.

Sustainability Commitment

Our Ethiopian Yirgacheffe beans are Rainforest Alliance certified, ensuring they are grown using methods that support social, economic, and environmental sustainability. We pay premium prices to farmers, investing in their communities and sustainable farming practices.
